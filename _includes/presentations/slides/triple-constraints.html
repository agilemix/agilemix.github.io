* Iron Triangle or Triple Constraints * Too many degrees of freedom - beam can slide expand along it's lemgth but not drop down or slide off it's bearing * To a client as a developer, you have pick any two but I control the third * Euler diagram * Unlike Venn type diagrams, there is no “sweet spot” * Waterfall - fixed around scope what to deliver, include resources and time is at risk * Agile - fixed around time and resources which means scope is at risk but small time slices means that changes in scope can be easily accommodated.
