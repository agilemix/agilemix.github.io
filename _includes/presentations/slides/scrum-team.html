* Software development is a team sport * Basketball is a good analogy for a Scrum Team * Coach - Product Owner - has the vision - knows what needs to be done - represents the stakeholders * Players - Dev Team - play the actual game - Multi-skilled, play together - single unit * Ass Coach - ScrumMaster - in the background - helping Coach/Player win the game * Crowd - Customers
